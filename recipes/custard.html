Custard - A Delicious Sweet

Description of Custard

Custard is a creamy culinary creation made by blending milk or cream with eggs and sugar. It can be baked, as in crème brûlée, or stirred on the stove-top for variations like vanilla custard.

The result is a smooth, velvety texture that serves as a base for desserts such as pies, puddings, and pastries.
